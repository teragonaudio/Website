Welcome to Teragon Audio!

Here you will find an assortment of audio plugins, utilities, source code, and information for both musicians and developers alike.

 

In the Plugins section you will find AU/VST plugins for both Mac OSX and Windows platforms. The Utilities section, you will find other audio tools and utilities. All software from Teragon Audio is free and open-source.

In the Developers section you will find resources about programming your own audio plugins. This includes guides for getting started with plugin development, as well as articles on various aspects audio programming.

In the Performers section you will find articles and tutorials about music software and hardware, and how to create tools to help you perform better.
